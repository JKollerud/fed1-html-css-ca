Frequently Asked Questions

What materials are Rainydays jackets made of?

Our jackets are crafted from high-quality waterproof materials designed to withstand heavy rain while keeping you comfortable and dry.

How long does shipping usually take?

Standard shipping within Norway takes 3-5 business days. International orders may take 7-10 business days depending on location.

Can I return or exchange a jacket?

Yes! You can return or exchange any unworn item within 30 days of delivery. Simply contact our support for a return label.
